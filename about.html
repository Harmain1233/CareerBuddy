Our Mission

We built this website on behalf of all frustrated undergraduate students, looking for a place to find opportunities easily. There's no login, so all you have to do is click buttons that will lead you to your dream offer!

Our available internships are for the majors STEM, Liberal Arts and General Studies. We hope to add more for students of all majors.

Our Origins

As a team of undergraduate students, we too have faced the difficulties of the internship search. We aim to help undergraduate students find a safe space, where they can feel at ease when searching for internships.
